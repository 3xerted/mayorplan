Mayoral Plan

Free Public Auto-Farms

Build efficient, free-to-use auto-farms for crops, food, and mob drops.

All origins are welcome, designed with accessibility in mind.

Origin-Friendly Infrastructure

  • Fallen Angels – No torches in designated areas to prevent nausea.
  • Merlings – Access to water highways and aquatic zones for safe, fast travel.
  • Enderians – Public water-free zones for protection and comfort.
  • Avian & Small Origins – Low-ceiling travel paths and perch points.
  • Tall Origins – Roads and bases will follow a minimum height guideline.

Public Transportation System

Construct a reliable minecart network along all major roads.

Includes stops at roads, major buildings, etc.

Rule Enforcement

  • Theft remains strictly illegal.
  • Griefing continues to be prohibited.

Origin Discrimination-Free Law

Enforce a strict Zero Discrimination Policy against any origin.

No player shall be excluded, insulted, or limited based on their:

  • Size (e.g., Shulk, Avian, Inchling, etc.)
  • Abilities or Limitations (e.g., Elytra-based flight, fire resistance, water dependency)
  • Combat style or mobility

Punishable offenses include:

  • Targeted bullying based on origin
  • Refusing access to public services based on origin
  • Designing traps or “origin-exclusion zones” meant to harm specific races
